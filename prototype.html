Help & FAQ
How do I record?
Tap the mic button and speak your update. Tap again to stop.
What if microphone access is denied?
Go to your browser settings and allow mic access for this page (usually the lock icon in the address bar).
Can I edit my report?
Yes — after generating, you'll see a preview where you can edit any field before submitting.
How long can my recording be?
At least 5 seconds. There's no maximum limit.
Who can see my report?
Your manager can see all submitted reports.
